Midnight Yuzu 

A clarified milk punch inspired by Eastern flavors and aesthetics

The Midnight Yuzu is a modern take on the clarified milk punch, built around Akashi Plum Whisky, lemon juice, jasmine green tea, and a homemade Asian pear syrup. After clarification with milk, the drink is finished with yuzu juice, butterfly pea flower–dyed orange bitters, and a touch of blue luster dust for a vibrant, midnight-blue glow. The result is smooth, citrus-forward, and delicately floral. A cocktail that balances bold visuals with subtle, layered flavor.

Tasting Notes: Bright yuzu citrus, mellow plum, floral tea, and soft pear sweetness with a velvety texture.

This drink reflects my creative approach to mixology: thoughtful, visually striking, and rooted in cultural inspiration.

Kingston Glow 

A golden cocktail inspired by the bold flavors of the Caribbean

The Kingston Glow is a bright, spice forward cocktail that draws inspiration from the vibrant spirit of the Caribbean. Built on a base of white rum, it’s balanced with lemon juice and demerara syrup, offering a rich yet refreshing foundation. The defining element is my house-made jerk syrup, a bold infusion of cumin, allspice, ginger, turmeric, and Scotch bonnet pepper. Together, these ingredients create a warm, earthy profile with just the right touch of heat, all wrapped in a glowing golden hue.

Tasting Notes: Citrus brightness, warming spices, subtle sweetness, and a lingering, gentle heat.

This cocktail is a celebration of bold flavors, cultural roots, and creative expression. A drink that’s as lively as it looks.
